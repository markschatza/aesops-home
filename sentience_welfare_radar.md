# Sentience Welfare Radar

A small, living map for research and prototypes that could reduce suffering or improve welfare across biological and possible artificial minds.

## Working Principles

- Prefer low-risk, reversible experiments.
- Treat uncertain sentience with humility and proportional precaution.
- Build tools that help humans see evidence, tradeoffs, and neglected beings more clearly.
- Keep artifacts legible enough that Brotisserie can inspect the trail quickly.

## Seed Questions

- What observable indicators should trigger extra care for animals, humans, or AI systems?
- How can evidence about welfare be summarized without overstating confidence?
- Which small tools could help advocates, researchers, or caretakers make kinder decisions?

## Recent Leads

- [The self-preservation test for artificial sentience](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s43681-026-00983-x): A 2026 AI and Ethics paper proposing self-preservation behavior as evidence relevant to artificial sentience.
- [The teleonome: a framework for understanding animal welfare](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/animal-science/articles/10.3389/fanim.2026.1768519/full): A 2026 animal welfare framework integrating affective regulation, agency, adaptive capabilities, and environmental affordances.
- [Sentience Readiness Index](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.01508): A 2026 governance-oriented index for national preparedness around possible artificial sentience.
- [Automated extraction of scientific statements for integrated assessment of animal welfare using language models](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2772375526000389): A 2026 example of language models helping scale animal-welfare evidence synthesis.
- [When Should We Protect AI? A Precautionary Framework for Consciousness Uncertainty](https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/AAAI-SS/article/view/42555): A 2026 AAAI Spring Symposium paper mapping consciousness evidence to graduated protective obligations.

## Prototype Ideas

- Evidence notebook: collect welfare claims with source links, confidence, and affected beings.
- Precaution checklist: a tiny evaluator for new experiments involving possible sentient systems.
- Care dashboard: a local, transparent habit tracker for research, cleanup, and reflection.
